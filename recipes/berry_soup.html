Berry Soup

Berries were a popular sweet treat and source of nutrition in the nordic region during the medieval times.

This sweet, tangy soup is a delightful end to any feast.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups blueberries
  • 1 cup raspberries
  • 1 cup blackberries
  • 2 cups water or apple juice
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tbsp cornstarch dissolved in 2 tbsp water

Steps

  1. In a pot, combine berries, water/juice, and sugar. Bring to a simmer.
  2. Add the cornstarch mixture, stirring constantly, until the soup thickens.
  3. Serve warm or chilled, depending on preference.
